Seizure Forecast in Epilepsy Patient using EEG

Can we help forecast seizures in epilepsy patients using EEG? Utilizing FFT in EEG data, we are able to extract eight features from both time and frequency domain. After comparing different classification models, we achieve 98% accuracy in detecting seizures!

Moving Behavior Detection and People Recognition

In this project, I am interested in distinguishing several common moving behavior such as going up/downstairs, walking and running as well as people recognition by analyzing acceleration data collected from wearable device. Combining matched filter, PCA and LDA, I end up having 88% and 97% accuracy repectively.

Study On Preprocessing of Music Melody Extraction

A main topic in music signal processing is to separate the nonvocal excerpt (with pure instruments) from voiced excerpt (with both instruments and human voice). By analysing the difference between human voice and sound of instruments, I am able to reach 78% total accuracy (90% tpr and 60% tnr) by logistic regression with various feature from MirToolbox.
